Our Story

From Seoul's streets to Delhi's heart

Born from a Love for Ramyun

Mr. K Ramyun Cafe was born from a simple obsession — the perfect bowl of ramyun. What started as late-night cravings and a passion for Korean street food culture evolved into Hauz Khas Village's favorite comfort food destination.

We believe that a great bowl of noodles has the power to heal, connect, and bring joy. Every dish at Mr. K is prepared using authentic Korean recipes, imported gochujang and doenjang, and locally sourced fresh produce. No shortcuts, no compromises — just honest, soulful food.

Our team of chefs has trained in Korean culinary traditions, ensuring that every spoonful of broth carries the depth of flavor that makes Korean food so addictive. From the first slurp of our spicy ramyun to the last grain of bibimbap rice — you'll taste the difference.
