Our Story

Dedicated to bringing out the radiant beauty in every woman. Geeta's Salon is more than just a beauty parlour; it's a sanctuary of luxury and transformation.

Founded with a passion for high-end beauty care, Geeta's Salon has become synonymous with luxury and perfection in Pilanji, New Delhi. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where state-of-the-art beauty treatments are delivered with a personal touch.
