Who We Are

The Art of Fitness

Aesthetix Fitness is a newly opened boutique fitness studio in Delhi, purpose-built for group classes that energize, strengthen, and inspire. We believe fitness should be a communal experience — challenging yet deeply rewarding.

Our diverse class lineup covers everything from the rhythm of Zumba to the discipline of Taekwondo. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, there's a class that fits your fire.
