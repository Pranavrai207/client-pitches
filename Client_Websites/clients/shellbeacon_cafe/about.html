Our Story

Built on the principles of luxury, taste, and an unforgettable ambiance, ShellBeacon is Hauz Khas Village's hidden gem.

Our Vision

A Sanctuary Above the City

At ShellBeacon Cafe, Bar & Kitchen, we believe dining is an art form. Our 3rd-floor location offers panoramic views of the serene Deer Park, juxtaposed with the lively energy of Hauz Khas Village.

We designed our space to seamlessly transition from a sun-drenched cafe serving artisanal roasts during the day, to an intimately lit, sophisticated lounge and bar at night. Every corner, from our plush seating to our patio lights, has been curated for your ultimate comfort.
